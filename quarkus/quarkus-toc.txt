Total Duration: 24 hours (3 Days)

Quarkus with Micropofile and Kubernetes:
Prerequisites: Java-17, Docker, Kubernetes (Docker and minikube)

1) Introdution
  a) Quarkus
  b) MicroProfile
  c) Kubernetes
2) Hello Quarkus (Develop your first Microservice with Quarkus)
3) Database Access(Panache, JPA, Active Record, Repository)
4) REST client(CDI, Programmatic)
5) Resiliency (Circuit Breaker, Bulkhead, Fallback, Timeouts, Retry)
5) Observability (Jaeger, Metrics, Prometheus, Grafana)
6) Security (OIDC, JWT, Keycloak)
Additional Topics:
7) Kafka with Smallrye
8) Deployment - (discuss strategies - JVM, Container, Micro-Container)
9) Installation (requires Docker, Kubernetes(Minikube), Java)
